Topic: Introduction to CAD software, fundamental tools, and sketching basics.

My Learning: My first day was all about getting acquainted with the software's interface. My Learning: This day focused on precision. I delved into more complex sketching techniques, using splines, patterns, and mirroring to create intricate designs. I also learned the crucial role of constraints—making sure my drawings were stable and editable for future modifications.
